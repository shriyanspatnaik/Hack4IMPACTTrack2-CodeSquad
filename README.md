## Problem Statement
In current healthcare systems, especially in high patient-load environments, doctors face significant challenges in efficiently prioritizing patients based on severity, while also ensuring continuous monitoring and treatment adherence. The absence of structured triage mechanisms leads to delayed attention for critical cases, and lack of effective follow-up systems results in poor medication compliance among patients. Additionally, communication gaps and unstructured patient data increase the burden on doctors and reduce overall quality of care.
